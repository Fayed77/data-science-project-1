This might suggest Republicans overall has more votes per county but also, notice that Democrats have a higher standard deviation of votes; which suggests their votes are more spread across in different counties rather than a single county. So, Biden (Democrat Party) has more votes on his side. This might suggest that Republicans are more responsible for the higher rate of infections.

Looking at the two graphs above, we can see they are polar opposites of each. This is because almost 98.1 percent of all total votes in the Unites States were made to Democrat and Republican Party; which also was almost at a 50/50 proportion. Observing from the first graph, theres a higher frequency of infections that occurred at lower percentages when voting for Democrats. But when voting for Republicans in the second graph there were more frequent infections at higher percentages. One thing this observation can suggest is that, when Republican supporters voted in higher amounts, they were less careful about safety protocols; because the more they voted the denser the second plot was.
